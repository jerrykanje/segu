Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive branding and signage solutions including custom signage, brand strategy, visual identity design, web development, graphic design, printing services, and installation.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple projects may take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive branding initiatives can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all signage products. Our experienced team ensures proper mounting, positioning, and setup for maximum visibility and impact.

Can you work with my existing brand?

Absolutely! We can work within your existing brand guidelines or help refresh and evolve your brand to better meet your current business objectives.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Greenhills, Randfontein, South Africa, and serve businesses throughout the region. Contact us to discuss your location and project requirements.
